What Buildr Does

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Buildr covers every workflow from site to office built specifically for how Indian construction businesses actually operate.

Material Management — Indent to GRN in one flow

Your site engineer raises a material indent on the app. Office approves it, converts to a Purchase Order, and when materials arrive, logs a GRN — all in one system. No WhatsApp back-and-forth. No lost invoices.

  • Real-time inventory tracking across all sites
  • Automated PO generation from approved indents
  • GRN logging with photo proof on mobile
  • Vendor payment tracking and reminders

Daily Progress Reports — auto-generated

Site engineers log progress and upload photos from their phone. DPRs are generated automatically and shared with clients. No formatting, no printing, no chasing.

  • 1-click DPR and WPR generation
  • Live site photos with geotag
  • Instant client-ready reports

Labour & Attendance Tracking

Track attendance for labour and staff from the mobile app. See who's on site, calculate payables, and get a daily headcount across all your projects at once.

  • Mobile attendance marking
  • Labour payable calculation
  • Contractor-wise breakdown

Budget & Cost Tracking

Set detailed budgets per project phase. Track every rupee spent in real time. Get instant alerts when spending is approaching limits — before it's too late.

  • Phase-wise budget setup
  • Real-time expense tracking
  • Variance alerts and reports

Task Management

Assign tasks to site engineers and track completion status from your phone or desktop. Know exactly what's done, what's blocked, and what's overdue — across every project.

  • Task assignment with deadlines
  • Blocker notifications instantly
  • Progress tracking per task
